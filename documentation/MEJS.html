string render(<string> content, <object> options)
    Calls the EJS rendering library. Templates are given the MEJS (Mobius Embeded JavaScript) extension to prevent overlap with Express' own EJS paraser.
    Parameters:
      content - content to render.
      options - options for renderer including variables for local scope of template.
    Returns:
      content parsed by the EJS engine.
